Case Study

Sugam Action Tracker — Centralized Task & Project Automation

Client Sugam Group — diversified logistics & operations business, Delhi NCR

A 100+ person organization running entirely on WhatsApp groups and Excel trackers. Zero visibility for leadership. No accountability. Decisions made in meetings disappeared before they could be acted on.

We built Sugam Action Tracker — a multi-tenant SaaS platform with role-based access across 5 levels, automatic MOM-to-task pipeline, real-time notifications via Email, Telegram and WhatsApp, full project tracking with phases and budgets, and an immutable audit log. Deployed on Supabase with a .NET 8 backend and React 19 frontend.

How we work

No big-bang deliveries.
No disappeared agencies.

We ship in short cycles, show our work weekly, and stay accountable throughout. You always know where your project stands.

01

Discovery

We spend time understanding your business — how decisions are made, where things break, and what success actually looks like for your team. This isn't a requirements gathering call. It's us doing our homework.

  • Business process walkthrough
  • Technical architecture scoping
  • Fixed-price proposal within 5 days
02

Design & Architecture

Before a single line of code, we map out user flows, data models, and integration points. You sign off on the blueprint before we build it — no surprises later.

  • Wireframes and UI prototypes
  • Database and API design
  • Role and permission architecture
03

Build in Sprints

We work in 2-week sprints with a live demo at the end of each one. You can try the software, give feedback, and redirect us — before we've gone too far in the wrong direction.

  • Weekly progress updates
  • Staging environment from day one
  • Continuous integration and testing
04

Launch & Onboarding

We handle deployment, data migration, and user onboarding. We train your team, write user guides, and stay on-call for the first 30 days post-launch. No "good luck, here's the repo."

  • Cloud deployment and DNS setup
  • User training sessions
  • 30-day hypercare support
05

Ongoing Support

We offer maintenance retainers for bug fixes, feature additions, and platform updates. Most clients stay with us after launch — because the system keeps evolving with the business.

  • Monthly retainer options
  • Dedicated support channel
  • Feature roadmap collaboration
