The Problem

Sound familiar?

These are the operational headaches we were built to solve.

💬

Tasks live in WhatsApp groups

Decisions are made in a meeting, then buried in a chat thread. By next week, nobody remembers who was supposed to do what.

"Bhai yeh kab hoga?" — every manager, every Monday
📊

Excel sheets no one updates

Someone creates a tracker. Within two weeks it's stale, version-controlled by three different people, and completely unreliable.

"The sheet was last updated 3 weeks ago…"
👻

Zero visibility for leadership

The CXO has no way to know which tasks are delayed, who's overloaded, or why a project slipped — without calling someone.

"I had to call 4 people just to find the status."
🔁

No accountability loop

Tasks get assigned but never followed up. No reminders. No escalations. No record of what was done or when.

"I thought Rahul was doing it. He thought I was."

Platform Features

Everything your operations team needs. Nothing they don't.

🗂️ Core

Task & Project Management

Create tasks, assign owners, set deadlines, track progress, and manage multi-task projects — all in one place. Bulk import via CSV or Excel.

📋 Core

Structured MOM Capture

Record meeting minutes in a standard format. Action points auto-convert to assigned tasks so nothing falls through the cracks after a meeting.

👥 Control

Role-Based Access & Hierarchy

Managers see their team. CXOs see their division. Admins see everything. Access and visibility mirror your actual org structure.

📈 Visibility

Live Dashboards

At-a-glance view of task status, overdue items, department performance, and completion rates. Drill down from company level to individual level.

🔔 Automation

Reminders & Escalations

Automatic reminders via Email, Telegram, and WhatsApp. Overdue tasks escalate to managers. Red-flag alerts surface at-risk work before deadlines are missed.

🛡️ Compliance

Audit Trail & Evidence

Every action logged. Attach files or links as evidence of completion. Full history of who changed what, when, and why. Reviewable any time.
